Workshops and Seminars

Membership Drive

The IEEE Membership drive for the year 2019-2020 started on 26th August 2019.
IEEE Committee members explained the benefits of IEEE membership to all the students during the publicity.

Talk on Artificial Intelligence

The seminar was taken by Ms. Deepthi Anne.
The seminar introduced various parts of Artificial Intelligence to the students, it included Q&A session as well.

Data Visualization Workshop

The workshop helped student get familiar with the basics of python, data structures in python.
The second part included various data visualization methods such as matplotlib, seaborn, plotly.

Handover Ceremony

The Core committee of 2018-19 was felicitated by the Core committee of 2019-20.
The members of Core committee 2018-19 shared their experience's with others.

AISYWC 19

Some members from IEEE-VIT represented IEEE-VIT at AISYWC-19 held at CMR institute, Hyderabad. The congress gave team an opportunity to network with other present in the congress.

IEEE day celebration

For celebrating IEEE day, we conducted a quiz competiton for all students of college.
The winners were felicitated and then the team had a cake cutting session.

Photoshop Workshop

The workshop helped students get familiar with the basics of Adobe Photoshop.
The second session included Image manipulation techniques and many more.

STEM Event

Some members of IEEE-VIT team visited a school and guided the students about the STEM.
We also conducted various experiments such as Refraction of light, Vedic Mathematics, etc.
